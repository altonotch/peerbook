Greetings & Salutations!

Welcome to peerbook a service for a private address book of servers and clients. peerbook started as yet-another-WebRTC-signaling-server and grew to a protocol and a system that lets you never key another IP address or copy a key ever again.

Changes to the address book are authorzied on a webpage with an "Update" button at the bottom. Above it, there's a list of peers name, fingerprint, kind and a checkbox to [un]verify. This page works only when the url ends with a valid token. Redis helps us ensure, tokens live for just 5 minute. A link with the token is emailed to the user whenever a new peer wants to authenticate [TODO: 2FA].

To review your peer list, please enter your email and we will forge and send a token.
